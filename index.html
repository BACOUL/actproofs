Evidence of authorized automated actions

ActProofs provides cryptographic evidence that an automated action was explicitly authorized by a known actor or organization, in a defined context, at a specific point in time.

What ActProofs is

Authorization proof

A standardized, portable proof of an authorization decision.

Compliance evidence

Designed for audits, governance, and regulatory accountability.

Independent verification

Verifiable without trusting internal systems or vendors.

Mental model

Identified actor → Authorization → ActProof → Action (out of scope)

Typical use cases

AI agent action approval
Automated workflows in regulated systems
Audit & liability documentation

What ActProofs is not

Not execution
Not IAM
Not logging
