The problem

No proof of intent

Execution logs do not prove authorization.

How it works

1. Declare

Describe the intended action locally.

2. Approve

Receive a signed authorization proof.

3. Verify

Verify publicly or offline, anytime.

Legitimate use cases

AI & Agents

Automated workflows

Clear separation between decision and execution.
