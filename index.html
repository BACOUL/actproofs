Proof of Authorized Automated Actions

ActProofs provides a cryptographic, portable proof that a specific automated action was explicitly authorized by a known actor or entity, in a defined context, at a precise time.

The missing proof layer

Logs
Mutable, internal, non-opposable
IAM / RBAC
Access control, not proof of decision
Execution traces
After-the-fact, incomplete

What ActProofs is

A standardized authorization proof format
A cryptographic receipt, portable and durable
Independently verifiable, stateless by design

What ActProofs is not

Not IAM, RBAC, or identity management
Not execution or workflow automation
Not logging or blockchain

Mental model

Identified actor → Authorization → ActProof → Action (out of scope)

Who it is for

AI governance & compliance teams
Audit, risk, and accountability functions
Regulated automation environments

Project status

Open draft specification · Reference implementation in progress · v0.1
