Automation created a responsibility gap

AI systems already trigger actions with real-world impact. Logs and permissions explain systems internally — they do not provide independent proof.

What ActProofs is

ActProofs is a standard and API that produces a cryptographically verifiable receipt proving that a specific automated action was explicitly authorized.

Why now

Automation is scaling faster than accountability. Regulation is catching up. Authorization must become provable.
