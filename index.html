Evidence of Authorized Actions

ActProofs provides cryptographic, portable, and independently verifiable proof that an automated or AI-driven action was explicitly authorized.

Read the ActProofs Standard Regulatory context

The problem

Implicit decisions

Automation executes actions without provable authorization.

Logs are not proof

Execution traces do not demonstrate intent or approval.

Audit failure

After incidents, authorization cannot be independently verified.

How it works

1. Declare

Describe the intended automated action.

2. Authorize

Issue a cryptographic authorization proof.

3. Verify

Verify the proof anytime, independently.

What ActProofs is not

❌ Execution engine
❌ Identity system
❌ Access control
