Limits & Threat Model

‚ö†Ô∏è Crucial Distinction: Authorization ‚â† Execution

ActProofs certifies that an intent was authorized by a valid key at a specific time. It does not guarantee that the external system executed the action successfully, nor does it enforce the action.

The verification process is technical (mathematical signature validity), not a legal judgment of the content. ActProofs is an audit layer, not an execution engine.

Privacy by Design

Our architecture prioritizes data minimization. We do not need to see your data to certify it.

Client-Side Hashing

Your manifest is hashed within your infrastructure. The ActProofs API never sees the raw content (prompts, PII, financial data).

Zero-Knowledge Issuance

We sign the cryptographic hash. We cannot reverse-engineer your business logic or sensitive parameters from the proof.

Stateless Verification

Verification is performed offline using the public key. No database lookups or API calls are required to verify a receipt.
